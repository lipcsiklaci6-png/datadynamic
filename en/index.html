The Architect

László Lipcsik

ESG Data Process Architect

CSRD and ESRS E1 have opened a new era in corporate reporting, but most companies' data infrastructure is still stuck in the past. My work bridges this gap. As a Data Engineer and ESG expert, I transform raw, unstructured corporate data streams (ERP, CSV, supplier dumps) into audit-ready, Big4-compliant reporting packages.

During my studies at Cambridge (CISL) – ESG Risk Management, Business Sustainability Management, and Sustainable Finance – I gained insights into the strategic and financial sides of sustainability. Combining this knowledge with my technical background (Rust, data modeling) enables the operation of a deterministic data refinery engine, the DataDynamic Refinery, which covers everything from Scope 1-3 emissions to financed emissions according to PCAF 2025, while guaranteeing audit-trail integrity with SHA-256 chaining and digital signatures.

Working with boutique consultancies in the German-speaking market, my mission is to take the burden of ESG data processing off their shoulders and provide ready, validated data packages that their clients can immediately sign off on. The data is mine, the decision is theirs.
